## Why this matters

Internal links help readers continue learning and help crawlers understand which
pages are important.

## Setup

Add links from new posts to older foundational posts. Use category pages and
related post sections as automatic linking layers.

## Implementation notes

Avoid generic anchor text like "click here". Link with phrases that describe the
target topic, such as "Astro sitemap setup" or "GitHub Pages deployment".

## Final checklist

- Each post links to related content
- Anchor text is descriptive
- Archive pages are linked globally
