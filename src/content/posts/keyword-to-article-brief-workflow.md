## Why this matters

A keyword is not enough to write a useful article. A brief turns search intent
into a clear publishing plan.

## Setup

Capture the target keyword, reader problem, article type, suggested title,
outline, internal links, and metadata notes.

## Implementation notes

Use the brief to decide what not to include. Focused articles are easier to rank
and easier to update later.

## Final checklist

- Intent is explicit
- Outline matches the query
- Internal links are planned
