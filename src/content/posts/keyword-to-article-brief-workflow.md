A keyword is not a content assignment. Before drafting, it must justify a unique reader outcome and evidence that the existing site does not already provide.

## The implementation boundary

Record the query, intent, reader state, promised result, required demonstration, code or workflow, limitations, internal-link targets, and the closest existing article. Merge when the new brief cannot stay distinct.

For Turn a Keyword into an Evidence-Ready Article Brief, the useful boundary is the smallest complete result that the repository can demonstrate. Supporting details stay in this guide when separating them would create a weaker URL, and claims stop where the saved output stops.

## What happened in this site

The 50-to-22 map is a real editorial case: provider variations, small components, and isolated checklists were absorbed into stronger guides instead of being rewritten as separate pages.

The keyword-to-article-brief-workflow case records what happened in AstroSEO.io rather than presenting one repository as a universal benchmark. Apply this content workflow check to your own URL model and compare the observed output before adopting the recommendation.

## A reliable working sequence

1. Define what a successful Turn a Keyword into an Evidence-Ready Article Brief result should contain.
2. Run `sed -n '1,80p' docs/content-consolidation-map.md` against the current production build.
3. Save only the output relevant to keyword-to-article-brief-workflow and redact private account data.
4. Compare the observed content workflow artifact with the intended inventory.
5. Make one focused correction, rebuild, and repeat this keyword-to-article-brief-workflow check.

Using the same keyword-to-article-brief-workflow measurement before and after a change separates diagnosis from guesswork and leaves a defensible rollback point.

## Limitations and recommendation

Prioritize evidence readiness over nominal search volume. A lower-volume problem with a reproducible answer is a better early asset than a broad term supported only by summaries.

For keyword-to-article-brief-workflow, automate the objective check shown above and keep the final usefulness decision with an editor. Static generation makes this content workflow output inspectable, but it cannot decide whether the explanation is distinct enough to deserve its URL.
