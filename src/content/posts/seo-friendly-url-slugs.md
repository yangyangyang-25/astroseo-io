## Why this matters

URLs are not just technical identifiers. They are visible in search results,
browser bars, analytics reports, and internal links.

## Setup

Use lowercase words separated by hyphens. Remove filler words when they do not
help meaning.

## Implementation notes

Do not change slugs casually after publishing. If a slug must change, add a
redirect on platforms that support it and update internal links.

## Final checklist

- Slug describes the topic
- Slug is not too long
- Published slugs stay stable
