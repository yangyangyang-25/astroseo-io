## Why this matters

Small sites usually do not need complex crawl budget work, but duplicate and
broken URLs still waste discovery signals.

## Setup

Keep indexable pages useful and linked. Avoid creating tag pages for tags that
only repeat the same single article unless they serve navigation.

## Implementation notes

Review generated routes after build. Static generators can produce many archive
pages quickly, so taxonomy discipline matters as the site grows.

## Final checklist

- Broken routes are removed
- Taxonomies stay useful
- Sitemap contains real content pages
