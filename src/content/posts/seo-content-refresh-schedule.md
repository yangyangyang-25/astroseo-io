## Why this matters

As a blog grows, maintenance becomes part of the publishing system. Ignoring old
content creates stale advice and weak internal links.

## Setup

Assign refresh frequency by importance. Core guides may need quarterly review,
while narrow tutorials can be reviewed twice a year.

## Implementation notes

Track refresh work in the same calendar as new posts. This prevents maintenance
from being treated as optional cleanup.

## Final checklist

- Important posts have review dates
- Refreshes are tracked
- Updates improve reader value
