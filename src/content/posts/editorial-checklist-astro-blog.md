## Why this matters

Publishing quality comes from repeatable checks, not from hoping every article
is handled consistently.

## Setup

Check title, description, headings, slug, category, tags, internal links, code
examples, and final checklist.

## Implementation notes

Keep the checklist close to the writing workflow. If writers draft in Obsidian,
put the checklist in the note template.

## Final checklist

- Metadata is complete
- Links are relevant
- Article answers the target intent
