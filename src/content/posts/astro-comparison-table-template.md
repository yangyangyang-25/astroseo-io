## Why this matters

Comparison posts often need tables, but wide tables can break mobile layouts if
they are not planned.

## Setup

Use concise columns and responsive wrappers. Keep table headings descriptive and
avoid packing paragraphs into cells.

## Implementation notes

For complex comparisons, split the article into sections after the table. The
table should summarize differences, not replace explanation.

## Final checklist

- Table works on mobile
- Columns are concise
- Follow-up explanation is included
