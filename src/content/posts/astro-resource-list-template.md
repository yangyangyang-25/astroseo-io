## Why this matters

Resource lists can become useful hub pages when they are curated instead of just
dumping links.

## Setup

Group resources by task, such as setup, deployment, metadata, and maintenance.
Add one sentence explaining why each link matters.

## Implementation notes

Review resource pages on a schedule. Outdated links damage trust faster on hub
pages because readers expect them to be maintained.

## Final checklist

- Resources are grouped by task
- Each link has context
- Page has a review cadence
