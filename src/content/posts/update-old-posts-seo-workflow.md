## Why this matters

Old posts lose value when examples, tool names, links, or recommendations become
stale.

## Setup

Review search intent, update outdated steps, improve examples, repair links, and
add internal links to newer related posts.

## Implementation notes

Change the modified date when the update is meaningful. Do not mark cosmetic
edits as major refreshes.

## Final checklist

- Facts and screenshots are current
- Broken links are fixed
- New internal links are added
