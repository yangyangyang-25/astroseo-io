## Why this matters

Evergreen posts create compounding value when they answer stable questions and
remain maintained over time.

## Setup

Choose topics with durable search intent. Prefer tutorials, checklists, and
reference pages over short-lived news unless the site strategy needs news.

## Implementation notes

Add a review cadence to each important post. Refresh examples, screenshots, and
links when the underlying tools change.

## Final checklist

- Topic has durable intent
- Article can be updated
- Internal links support the page
