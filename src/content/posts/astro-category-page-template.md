## Why this matters

Category pages are important discovery pages for both readers and crawlers.

## Setup

Generate one category route per category. Add a heading, short explanation, and
a sorted list of posts in that category.

## Implementation notes

Do not leave category pages as empty lists. A short intro helps clarify what the
archive covers and why the posts belong together.

## Final checklist

- Category pages have unique headings
- Posts are sorted by date
- Archive is linked from the homepage
