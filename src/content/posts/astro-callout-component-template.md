## Why this matters

Callouts help readers notice important implementation details without making the
whole article harder to scan.

## Setup

Create a component with a type, title, and content slot. Use a small set of
types such as note, tip, warning, and checklist.

## Implementation notes

Avoid overusing callouts. If every paragraph is highlighted, none of the
highlights feel important.

## Final checklist

- Types are limited
- Styling is readable
- Content remains scannable
