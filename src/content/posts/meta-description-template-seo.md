## Why this matters

A meta description does not guarantee rankings, but it shapes how a result is
understood in search and social previews.

## Setup

Use one sentence that names the topic, the audience, and the practical result.
Avoid repeating the title word for word.

## Implementation notes

For tutorials, describe the task and outcome. For checklists, describe what the
reader can validate. Keep descriptions specific enough to distinguish similar
posts.

## Final checklist

- Description is unique
- Search intent is clear
- Outcome is concrete
