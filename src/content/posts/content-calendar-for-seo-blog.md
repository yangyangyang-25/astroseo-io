## Why this matters

Without a calendar, content teams often publish scattered posts and forget
refresh work.

## Setup

Track keyword, category, draft owner, publish date, status, and refresh date.
Keep the calendar simple enough to maintain weekly.

## Implementation notes

Group posts by cluster instead of publishing isolated topics randomly. This
creates stronger internal linking and clearer topical coverage.

## Final checklist

- Calendar includes refresh dates
- Topics map to clusters
- Publishing cadence is realistic
