## Why this matters

Clusters help a site cover a topic in depth instead of publishing disconnected
articles.

## Setup

Choose a pillar topic, list supporting questions, and map each support post back
to the pillar with contextual links.

## Implementation notes

Use categories for broad navigation and tags for narrower relationships. Avoid
creating a new taxonomy label for every small keyword variation.

## Final checklist

- Pillar topic is clear
- Supporting posts answer subtopics
- Internal links connect the cluster
