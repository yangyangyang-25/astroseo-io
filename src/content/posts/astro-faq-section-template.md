## Why this matters

FAQ sections can answer follow-up questions without interrupting the main flow
of a tutorial.

## Setup

Collect real questions from the topic and keep answers short. Place the FAQ
after the main content, not before the core explanation.

## Implementation notes

Do not add filler questions only for keywords. A good FAQ should reduce reader
confusion and improve article completeness.

## Final checklist

- Questions are relevant
- Answers are concise
- FAQ follows the main tutorial
