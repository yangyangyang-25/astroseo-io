## Why this matters

A clear template speeds publishing and keeps article structure consistent across
a growing content site.

## Setup

Include frontmatter, a problem statement, setup instructions, implementation
notes, and a final checklist.

## Implementation notes

Use the template as a guide, not a rigid script. Some posts need examples,
tables, or troubleshooting sections, while others can stay concise.

## Final checklist

- Frontmatter is complete
- Structure matches reader intent
- Checklist summarizes action items
